  Goals of this Project

Capstone Project -- COMP 4449 -- 11/12/2022


Combining both Meteorology with Data Science has been a focal point of my studies. Taking a difficult science full of prediction and forcasting, and applying it to a discipline that solves problems using prediction can lead to powerful results. The next steps involve building individual models from scratch, non-reliant on packages or extensions. Developing a model that produces high confidence while providing accurate results is the true goal but when dealing with extensive amounts of weather phenomena, overfitting becomes a serious issue.

Future goals aim at applying image classification into automotive, aviation and shipping industries to improve safety and reaction to changing weather situations. In cases of scientific research (such as Storm Chasing), having an application capable of assissting in interpretation can improve reaction when time is crucial.
